📖 About Tamilகதைகள்
தமிழ் கதைகளின் களஞ்சியம் — உங்கள் வரவேற்கிறோம்!

🌟 எங்களைப் பற்றி — Who We Are

Tamilகதைகள் (kathais.xyz) என்பது தமிழ் வாசகர்களுக்காக உருவாக்கப்பட்ட ஒரு இலவச கதை வாசிப்பு தளம். காதல், நகைச்சுவை, பயம், நீதி, குழந்தைகள் கதைகள் என்று அனைத்து வகையான கதைகளும் இங்கே கிடைக்கும்.

We are a free Tamil storytelling platform built for readers who love Tamil literature, short stories, and creative writing. Our mission is to keep Tamil stories alive and accessible to everyone — on any device, anytime, anywhere.

🎯 எங்கள் நோக்கம் — Our Mission

தமிழ் மொழியில் கதை வாசிக்கும் அனுபவத்தை ஒவ்வொரு வயதினருக்கும் எளிதாக வழங்குவது எங்கள் நோக்கம். அனைத்து கதைகளும் இலவசம் — எப்போதும், எப்பொழுதும்.

  • 1000+ Tamil short stories — free forever
  • Multiple categories: love, horror, comedy, kids, moral, devotion, thriller, history
  • Text-to-speech support for listening to stories
  • PWA — works offline on any device
  • User-submitted stories — anyone can publish
  • Daily Story, Leaderboard, Games, and more!

💡 தொழில்நுட்பம் — Technology

இந்த தளம் HTML, CSS, மற்றும் JavaScript மூலம் உருவாக்கப்பட்டது. Firebase Authentication மூலம் பாதுகாப்பான உள்நுழைவு, Google AdSense மூலம் தளத்தை இலவசமாக பராமரிக்கிறோம்.

This platform is built with pure HTML/CSS/JavaScript, Firebase Authentication, and Google AdSense. No subscription, no paywall — always free.

🙏 நன்றி — Thank You

எங்கள் தளத்தை படிக்கும் அனைத்து வாசகர்களுக்கும், கதை எழுதி பகிர்ந்த எல்லோருக்கும் மனமார்ந்த நன்றி! நீங்கள்தான் இந்த தளத்தின் உயிர்.

🔒 Privacy Policy
Last updated: January 2026 | உங்கள் தனியுரிமை எங்களுக்கு முக்கியம்

1. Introduction

Welcome to Tamilகதைகள் (kathais.xyz). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when you:

  • Sign in using Google OAuth 2.0 (name, email, profile photo)
  • Submit a story (story content, title, category)
  • Leave comments or reactions on stories
  • Contact us via our contact form

We also automatically collect certain technical data: browser type, device type, pages visited, time spent on pages, and approximate geographic location (country/city level only).

3. Google AdSense & Cookies

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our site. Google AdSense uses cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites on the internet. These cookies allow Google and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our site.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings. You can also opt out via the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page.

Third party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visits to this and/or other sites on the internet.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our service
  • To personalize your reading experience
  • To save your bookmarks, reading history, and preferences locally on your device
  • To display relevant advertisements via Google AdSense
  • To improve our website based on usage data
  • To respond to your inquiries and support requests

5. Data Storage

Most of your preferences (bookmarks, reading history, theme, font size) are stored locally in your browser's localStorage and never sent to our servers. Authentication data is handled securely by Google Firebase.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase — Authentication (login/signup)
  • Google AdSense — Advertisement display
  • Google Fonts — Typography (Tiro Tamil, DM Sans, etc.)

Each third party has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. Our kids' story section is designed to be family-friendly and safe.

8. Your Rights

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to delete your account and data
  • Right to opt out of personalized ads
  • Right to data portability

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@kathais.xyz.

9. Cookies Policy

We use essential cookies for login sessions and Google AdSense cookies for advertising. By continuing to use our site, you consent to our use of cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect certain features.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: hello@kathais.xyz
  • Website: kathais.xyz
